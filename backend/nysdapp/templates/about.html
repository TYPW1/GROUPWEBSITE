About ELECTRONYSD

Our Mission

At ElectroNysd, we are a group of dedicated students from ICT University. Our goal is to provide affordable electronic components like Arduino boards, breadboards, motors, and more to young innovators and educational institutions. We believe that by providing access to affordable tools, we can help students and schools with low capital to enhance their creativity and build the innovators of tomorrow.

Who We Are

Electronysd is a student-run initiative focused on making electronics accessible to all. We sat together with a shared vision to promote creativity in technology and education. Our products are designed to cater to hobbyists, schools, and organizations that want to invest in the future of innovation.

Why Choose Us?

  • Affordable electronics for all your project needs.
  • Quality products, including Arduino boards, motors, and more.
  • Support for schools with limited budgets.
  • A chatbot to help answer your project-related questions.

Get in Touch

Have questions? Our chatbot is available 24/7 to help students and schools ask questions about the right components for their projects. Whether you're working on a robotics project or something else, we're here to guide you!
